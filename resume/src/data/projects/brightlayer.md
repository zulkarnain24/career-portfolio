## Brightlayer — Eaton IoT Platform

**Role:** Senior Mobile Architect

A comprehensive IoT platform for power management and energy monitoring across industrial facilities.

**Impact:**

- Enabled 10,000+ industrial device deployments across APAC and 50k+ active users for monitoring and control.
- Improved field reliability and reduced incident response time through real-time alerts and diagnostics.

**Technical Highlights:**

- Architected a BLE Mesh protocol and deployment pipeline for scalable device provisioning and maintenance.
- Delivered a cross-platform mobile experience (React Native + native modules) with offline-first sync and strong data encryption.
- Designed telemetry pipelines for low-latency monitoring and secure cloud ingestion.
